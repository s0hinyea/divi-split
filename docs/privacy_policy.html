1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide

When you create a bill split, you may enter names or labels for the people involved (for example, "Alex" or "Sam"). This information is entirely optional and up to you — you can use nicknames, initials, or any label you like.

If you choose to send a payment notification, you may also enter a phone number or email address for each person. This is also voluntary. We never require it to calculate a split.

b) Information Collected Automatically

To keep Divi running smoothly, we collect limited technical information automatically:

  • Device type and operating system version (for debugging and compatibility)
  • Anonymized app usage data (such as how many scans you run or which features you use) to help us improve the app
  • Your IP address, which is a standard part of any network request

This data is not linked to your identity and is used solely for technical purposes.

c) Receipt Images (Camera Data)

When you scan a receipt, the image you capture is transmitted to OpenAI's API so that the text on the receipt can be extracted and parsed. This is the core function of the app.

Receipt images are processed by OpenAI. Please review OpenAI's privacy policy at openai.com/privacy.

Divi does not permanently store your receipt images on its own servers. Once OpenAI processes the image and returns the parsed data to us, the image itself is not retained by Divi.

Receipt images may incidentally contain personal data — such as names printed on receipts, restaurant names, or addresses. Divi takes no responsibility for the content that appears in user-generated scans. We recommend only scanning receipts from your own meals.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for specific, limited purposes:

  • To parse receipt data and calculate how to split the bill among your group
  • To send payment request notifications to the contacts you specify
  • To improve app performance, fix bugs, and enhance reliability
  • To maintain your receipt history so it's available across your devices

We do not use your data for advertising or to build a profile of you for marketing purposes.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties — ever.

3. Third-Party Services

Divi works with the following third-party services. Each one receives only the data necessary for its specific purpose.

OpenAI

Purpose: AI-powered receipt text extraction — this is how Divi reads and parses the items on your receipt.

What is shared: The receipt image you capture during each scan.

Divi uses OpenAI's API to process receipt images. As of 2025, Apple and Google require explicit disclosure when an app uses AI services to process user content. Per that requirement: Divi uses OpenAI to analyze your receipt images. Images are sent to OpenAI over an encrypted connection and are not retained by Divi after processing.

Privacy policy: openai.com/privacy

Supabase

Purpose: Secure cloud database, user authentication, and backend infrastructure. Supabase is how we store your account information and give you access to your receipt history across devices.

What is shared: Your account details, saved receipt splits, and saved contact names.

Privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy

Apple App Store & Google Play Store

Divi is distributed through Apple's App Store and the Google Play Store. These platforms may collect their own data as part of the standard app download and update process. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy for details.

4. Data Retention

  • Receipt images: Not retained by Divi after OpenAI processes the scan. The image is ephemeral — it exists only long enough to be analyzed.
  • Split data: Your bill splits are stored securely in our cloud database (Supabase) so you can view your receipt history and access it across all your devices.
  • Contact info (names and numbers): Saved to your account in our database so that assigning future splits is faster. You can delete individual contacts from within the app at any time.
  • Account deletion: If you request that your account be deleted, all of your associated splits, contacts, and account data are permanently and irreversibly removed from our servers. To request deletion, contact us at [DEVELOPER_EMAIL].

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to anyone.

The only data sharing that occurs is what's necessary to operate the app:

  • We share receipt images with OpenAI solely for the purpose of text extraction
  • We use Supabase as our securely hosted cloud infrastructure to store account and split data

We may be required to disclose information if compelled by law — for example, in response to a valid court order, subpoena, or legal process. We will notify you of such requests to the extent permitted by law.

We work with no advertising networks and no data brokers.

6. Children's Privacy

Divi is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If we learn that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at [DEVELOPER_EMAIL] and we will take immediate steps to remove it.

Divi complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

8. Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Here is what we do:

  • All data transmitted between Divi and OpenAI's API uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Receipt images are sent over encrypted connections only
  • Your account and split data is stored in Supabase, which is secured with industry-standard practices
  • Divi does not collect, transmit, or store any payment information — ever

Despite our best efforts, no method of transmitting data over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to protecting your data using reasonable and industry-standard measures.
