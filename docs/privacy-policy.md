# Divi Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Divi helps users scan receipts, split expenses, and share payment details with friends. This policy explains what information the app processes and how it is used.

## Information We Collect

- Account information such as email address and profile details you choose to add, including username and payment handles.
- Receipt information you submit, including receipt images and the structured item, tax, tip, and total data extracted from those images.
- Usage data needed to keep the app working, such as authentication sessions and records of saved receipts.

## Contacts

Divi may request access to your device contacts so you can assign items to people more quickly. Contact data is used locally on your device for selection and messaging flows. Divi does not upload or store your raw device contact list on its backend.

## How We Use Information

- To create and manage your account.
- To process receipt images and extract line items for bill splitting.
- To save split history and profile settings.
- To generate payment summaries and optional payment links for sharing.
- To maintain app security, reliability, and fraud prevention controls.

## Third-Party Services

Divi uses third-party infrastructure providers to operate the service, including:

- Supabase for authentication and data storage.
- AI/OCR processing providers to analyze receipt images.
- Platform services from Apple, Google, and Expo where applicable.

These providers only receive the information needed to perform their service functions.

## Data Retention

We keep account and receipt history data for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

## Your Choices

- You can update your profile information inside the app.
- You can stop granting contact or photo permissions through your device settings.
- You can request account deletion by contacting support.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information. No system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## Children's Privacy

Divi is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date when changes are made.

## Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact: support@divi.app
